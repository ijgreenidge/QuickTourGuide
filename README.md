Are you traveling to a city you have never been too? Do you want to get a preview of that city? Look no further than your Quick Tour Guide.
Quick Tour Guide shows you photos and YouTube videos from your desired city. It also shows you location on the map. You get to see the culture even before you step foot in that city and get a good first impression.
